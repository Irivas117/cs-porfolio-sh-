What is Vindinium?

Vindinium is a game that requires the skills of a coder in order for the code to work. It is also a competetive game that requires a lot of skill in javascript. In the game you own your very own AI(artificial intelligence) and you compete againts other players. In the game there is a specific amount of turns which is about 1,200 turns. During the course of the game to becoming a victor your AI must be able to collect freemines and attacking other players for their gold. In the game freemines are a way to get gold and win the game. Inorder for you to win you must have the most gold at the end of the game.
